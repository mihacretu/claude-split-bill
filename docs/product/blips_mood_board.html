Split Bill App

Design System & Visual Identity

Design Principles

  • Glass Morphism: Translucent surfaces with subtle blur effects create depth and modern appeal
  • Soft Gradients: Gentle metallic gray gradients provide elegant backgrounds without overwhelming content
  • Subtle Depth: Layered shadows and borders create visual hierarchy while maintaining cleanliness
  • Musical Theme: Transform boring bill-splitting terms into fun musical language (Groups → Tracks, Scan → Hit Record)
  • Friendly Interaction: Warm accent colors and smooth animations make the app approachable

Design Philosophy

The Split Bill app embraces a clean, modern aesthetic with glass morphism elements and soft gradients. The design prioritizes usability while maintaining visual elegance through subtle shadows, thoughtful spacing, and a harmonious color palette that guides user interaction naturally.
